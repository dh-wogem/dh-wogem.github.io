Parenthood

DH-WOGEM provides a welcoming space to discuss challenges, frustrations, and needs with regard to becoming a parent in DH, and doing DH as a parent.

Whether you're thinking about having kids someday, trying to have kids, or are living the combination of DH and parenthood, you are welcome to join DH-WOGEM's calls (scheduled, typically, every three months) for community and commiseration. We also take on projects -- locally and within the broader DH community -- with the goal of supporting current and future parents.
